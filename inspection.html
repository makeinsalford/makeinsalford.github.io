Inspection & Reverse Engineering

Co-ordinate Measurement, Laser Scanning and Microscopy

Equipment
  • Nikon Altera 10108 CMM
  • Nikon CMM Arm
  • Nikon LC60Ds Laser Scanner Head
  • Zeiss Digital Microscope
  • Zeiss Optical Microscope

We have a range of equipment available to measure, prod and probe all sorts of parts. Either inspect parts made with other processes for conformity or reverse engineer features for inclusion in your own designs.

Optical and digital microscopes over up to magnification up to 1000x, and the motorised platten allows for automatic stitching of micrographs into a large high resolution canvases. Focus stacking capabilities allow for pseudo-3D scanning of minute features.
