The Maker Space's Print Hive, located in the Newton Building's basement is home to the majority of our 3D printing capacity. With specialist printers like Markforged's Mark 2 you can print with Nylon reinforced with continuous strands of carbon fibre or kevlar, and with Formlabs' Form 2 SLA machine high resolution, superb quality prototypes are at your fingertips.

Day to day printing requirements are handled by our fleet of Ultimaker 3D printers, ranging from the Ultimaker 2+ all the way up through to the latest S5 model. The Morson Engine Room, located on Newton's Ground Floor, contains the Big Rep printer which has a print volume of up to a cubic metre.

Markforged's Metal X system offers a safe method of 3D printing a wide variety of metals, ranging from 17-4 PH Stainless Steel to Copper and Inconel. Printing with an FDM process, parts are loaded into a sintering oven to result in 98%+ dense solid metal parts which can be processed further.
